Public Smiling Hours Extended Through Winter

By Staff Correspondent — Ministry of Wellbeing Desk · October 24, 2025

Nationwide grin initiative credited with “increased warmth and cheek resilience.” The Ministry of Wellbeing announced the official extension of Public Smiling Hours to preserve morale visibility through the darker quarter, increasing daily duration from fourteen to sixteen hours.
