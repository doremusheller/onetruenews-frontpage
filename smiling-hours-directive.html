“A nation that smiles together — thrives together. Grin for growth!”

From the windows above, families leaned outward in perfect alignment, faces glowing beneath the bluish inspection lamps. “It feels good to be noticed,” said one resident, beaming dutifully.

The Ministry projects a 12 percent increase in ambient happiness and expects “seasonally adjusted joy” to remain steady through spring.
