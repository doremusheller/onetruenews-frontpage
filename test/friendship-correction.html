Banners Repair Broken Diplomacy

Friendship Correction

Government outreach invites citizens to “reassess unhelpful relationships” for smoother patriotism.

Social Renewal Bureau · October 24, 2025

The pilot matches citizens with facilitators who help identify “friend friction” and replace it with “national rhythm.” Participants report improved loyalty, reduced doubt, and easier small talk at mandatory events.

Critics raised concerns about coerced civility. Administrators countered with charts, banners, and testimonials featuring unison nodding. “We’re not ending friendships,” a coordinator said. “We’re optimizing them.”

Next steps include neighborhood “Reconciliation Hours” where snacks are provided and dissent is workshopped into applause. Metrics look promising, especially the applause part.
